The article, "Best Practices for Modals/Overlays/Dialog Windows" describes some of the best practices when it comes to filling out online forms and filling out important text fields for surveys and documents. Best Practices for Form Design

October 14, 2024

Link to article

The article, "Best Practices for Form Design" is one of the most important things that we handle today. Everything is essentially becoming digitalized, so online forms are very popular nowadays. This article basically explains ways to make a very user-friendly platform for users to be able to easily fill out an online form and do it correctly. In a digital world, cognition load and feasability are two very important things when it comes to transferring analog items to an online platform. Having a red asterisk when it comes to required things, or not being able to leave a page until everything is filled out are both indicators in order for a person to fill out an online form.
